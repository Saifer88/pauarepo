# Product Overview

Personal/professional website for **Paola Mincuzzi**, a Personal Trainer based in Bari, Italy.

## Purpose
- Showcase services (Personal Training, Functional Assessment, Functional Recovery)
- Display credentials, certifications, and methodology
- Allow clients to purchase training packages via Stripe
- Collect inquiries via WhatsApp contact form
- Display client testimonials loaded from a JSON file

## Target Audience
Italian-speaking adults aged 35–60 in the Bari area seeking personal training, postural correction, kinesiology, or functional recovery services. People who want to get back in shape, prevent age-related issues, or recover from injuries/sedentary lifestyle.

## Key Features
- Single-page scrolling website with section-based navigation
- Hero section with CTA to packages and contact
- "Chi Sono" (About) with credentials and certifications
- Services section linking to pricing packages
- Animated gallery carousel
- Testimonial bubbles with auto-rotating highlight animation
- Pricing cards with Stripe checkout links
- WhatsApp-based contact form
- Cookie consent banner (GDPR)
- SEO optimizations (JSON-LD structured data, Open Graph, meta tags)

## Branding
- Accent color: warm gold/tan (`#d4a574`)
- Fonts: Playfair Display (headings), Inter (body)
- Hashtag: #teammincuzzi
- Language: Italian (lang="it")
